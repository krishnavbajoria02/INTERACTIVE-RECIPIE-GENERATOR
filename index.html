PAV BHAJI - A MUMBAI DELICACY

INGREDIENTS:

  • FOR THE PAV:
    • Pav
  • FOR THE BHAJI:
    • Potatoes
    • Carrots
    • Cauliflower
    • Peas
    • Onions
    • Tomatoes
    • Garlic
    • Ginger
    • Oil
    • Water
    • Salt
    • Pav Bhaji Masala
  • FOR GARNISHING:
    • Onions
    • Cilantro
    • Lemon

INSTRUCTIONS:

  1. PREPARATION:
    1. Boil the mixed vegetables until they are soft. Drain and mash them well.
    2. Boil the potatoes separately, peel them, and mash them as well.
    3. Keep the boiled and mashed vegetables aside.
    4. Slit the pav buns horizontally, keeping them connected on one side.
  2. COOKING THE BHAJI:
    1. Heat oil or butter in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
    2. Add minced garlic, ginger, and chopped green chilies. Sauté until aromatic.
    3. Add chopped onions and sauté until they turn translucent.
    4. Add the chopped bell peppers and cook for a few minutes until they start to soften.
    5. Add the chopped tomatoes and cook until they become soft and mushy.
    6. Add the boiled and mashed mixed vegetables and potatoes. Mix well.
    7. Add red chili powder, turmeric powder, pav bhaji masala, and salt. Mix everything thoroughly.
    8. Add the boiled and mashed vegetables (carrots and peas) and mix again.
    9. Use a potato masher or the back of a spoon to mash everything together. You want a slightly chunky consistency.
    10. Add a little water if the mixture is too dry. Let the bhaji simmer for about 10-15 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.
  3. TOASTING THE PAV:
    1. Heat a flat griddle or pan. Spread a little butter on the cut sides of the pav buns.
    2. Toast the pav buns on the griddle until they turn golden brown and slightly crispy.
  4. SERVING:
    1. Serve the hot and flavorful bhaji with the toasted pav buns.
    2. Garnish with finely chopped onions, chopped cilantro, and a squeeze of lemon juice.
    3. Serve additional butter on the side if desired.
    4. Enjoy the Pav Bhaji hot and relish the delicious combination of flavors and textures.
